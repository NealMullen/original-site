Legal solicitor website (concept work)

A small dynamic law firm recently got in touch with me about helping them to re-think their current website. Excited by the possibilities and ideas I had going through my mind, I decided to make a start without first winning the bid for the project.

Out of the 11 different designers, developers & agencies they got in touch with, they narrowed their choice down to me and one other agency. Unfortunately I didn't get the opportunity to work on this project for unknown reasons, but I decided to keep working on this as a personal project.

Current status

This project is still very much in development due to other work commitments. I will hopefully get this project finished when I have a little more time on my hands.
