What I like to focus on.

Building responsive experiences

Users should be given a great experience, no matter how they decide to view your website. This is where the beauty of responsive design comes in. Through the use of fluid grids and wizardry, your website can be built to respond and adapt to the needs of users, making it look good and function across a range of devices; from wide screen monitors to mobile phones.

Handcrafted, semantic coding

I love digging into code, which is why all of my work is handcrafted by yours truly; using templates or frameworks such as Bootstrap just isn't my thing. The semantics, accessibility and scalability of my code is at the forefront of my mind - I probably spend a little too much time trying to "perfect" these aspects - but you'll thank me in the end!

User interface design

A good looking interface always gets the thumbs up! But it isn't all about looks, great consideration to the UX is always given so that the interface both looks good and is suitable for your users and their goals. This process often starts with rough sketches and wireframes, leading into designing visual prototypes in Photoshop or designing straight in the browser.
